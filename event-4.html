Tips and Tricks:

Making it simple for Class II and Class III Treatment from Start to Finish

This full day lecture will focus on the clinical use of the Genius System passive self-ligating brackets. Orthodontists and general practitioners will find valuable and relevant clinical and scientific information to understand the specificity of this unique passive self-ligating system. Every participant should then be willing and able to start the next day on the use of the Genius System for easy and complex cases, for which a step by step approach will covered.
