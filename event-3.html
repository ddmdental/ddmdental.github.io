Orthodontic Management of Patients:

A Simple Approach from Start to Finish

Achieving harmony between the aesthetic needs and the demands of occlusion in growing and adult patients can be a tricky taskk. The difficulty in achieving optimum occlusion on patients with skeleto-dental malocclusions requires thorough assessment and an even more comprehensive treatment plan approach.

This one day seminar will focus on MECHANO-THERAPY PROTOCOL that is expected of a dentist who wishes to integrate simple orthodotic treatment in his/her busy practice. We will focus on the three (3) essential problems in malocclusion and find simple yet effective ways to treat such cases from start to finish.
